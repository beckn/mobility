# GTFS Schema List
This table presents a **comprehensive inventory of all schemas (nouns) defined by the GTFS family of standards**, spanning **GTFS Schedule (static)**, **GTFS Realtime**, **GTFS Flex**, and **GTFS Fares (v1 and v2)**. Each row corresponds to a **conceptual entity explicitly modeled by the standard**—whether represented as a CSV file, a GeoJSON artifact, or a Protobuf message—and lists the **properties that constitute its structural contract**, along with their data types and relationships. The intent of this inventory is not to reinterpret GTFS, but to **surface its full semantic surface area in a normalized, machine-readable form**, serving as a foundation for downstream tasks such as semantic binding to Beckn core schemas, JSON-LD context generation, validation tooling, and cross-standard interoperability analysis.
